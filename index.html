r/worldnews Temporal Sentiment Analysis

Explore public sentiment on global topics with dynamic visualizations and an interactive chat tool

What is this project about?

Reddit is a massive platform where people discuss everything from politics to pop culture. This project focuses on analyzing the sentiment of Reddit comments from the popular subreddit r/worldnews. By leveraging advanced sentiment analysis and interactive visualizations, we aim to provide insights into how people feel about key global topics such as Joe Biden, Ukraine, and more.

How to use this website?

Navigate through the site to explore interactive charts and visualizations:

  • Interactive Area Chart: Select a topic to see how sentiments like joy, anger, and approval changed over time.
  • Word Cloud: Click on an area chart section to view frequently mentioned words associated with specific emotions and topics.
  • Comment Table: Review detailed comments that highlight the selected sentiment.
  • Sentiment Chat: Use our chat interface to generate custom visualizations. Simply type your query, such as: "Create a line chart for Joe Biden, Trump, and Elon Musk for approval from January to June by week."

The tool is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, allowing anyone to uncover trends and patterns in public sentiment with just a few clicks.

Sentiment Chat: Interactive Data Requests

The Sentiment Chat allows you to dynamically generate visualizations based on your own queries. Simply enter a prompt specifying the topic, sentiment, and date range you want to explore, and the system will create an interactive chart tailored to your request. This feature is perfect for customizing your analysis and exploring public opinion on your terms.

For example, you can ask:

  • "Show me the weekly sentiment trends for Palestine and Ukraine from May to August."
  • "Create a bar chart comparing Joe Biden and Donald Trump for approval sentiment."
  • "Display a line chart for Elon Musk’s disapproval sentiment from January to December."

Whether you’re a researcher or a curious observer, this tool makes it easy to analyze Reddit sentiment dynamically.
